# Jungian Archetypes: Healthy & Shadow Expressions

## Core Structural Archetypes

### Self
- **Healthy:** Wholeness, integration, authenticity, psychological balance
- **Shadow:** Inflation (identifying as godlike), fragmentation, loss of ego boundaries

### Shadow
- **Healthy:** Integration of denied parts, self-awareness, depth
- **Shadow:** Projection onto others, denial, acting out repressed impulses

### Anima (feminine in men)
- **Healthy:** Emotional depth, creativity, intuition, connection
- **Shadow:** Moodiness, irrationality, manipulation, emotional volatility

### Animus (masculine in women)
- **Healthy:** Assertiveness, logic, independence, conviction
- **Shadow:** Rigidity, harsh judgment, dogmatism, cruel opinions

### Persona
- **Healthy:** Appropriate social adaptation, professional competence
- **Shadow:** Identification with role, phoniness, loss of authentic self

## Major Character Archetypes

### Innocent
- **Healthy:** Genuine faith, optimism, trust, childlike wonder, hope
- **Shadow:** Willful ignorance, denial, passivity, learned helplessness, magical thinking

### Orphan/Everyman
- **Healthy:** Realism, empathy, groundedness, humility, solidarity
- **Shadow:** Cynicism, victimhood, abandonment issues, powerlessness

### Hero
- **Healthy:** Courage, determination, competence, achievement, protecting others
- **Shadow:** Arrogance, need to prove worth constantly, ruthlessness, inability to rest

### Caregiver
- **Healthy:** Compassion, generosity, nurturing, selfless service
- **Shadow:** Martyrdom, enabling, codependency, guilt manipulation, self-neglect

### Explorer
- **Healthy:** Independence, authenticity, adventure, seeking fulfillment
- **Shadow:** Aimless wandering, inability to commit, perpetual dissatisfaction, escapism

### Rebel/Outlaw
- **Healthy:** Liberation, authentic change, challenging injustice, freedom
- **Shadow:** Destructiveness, criminal behavior, rebellion without purpose, nihilism

### Lover
- **Healthy:** Passion, intimacy, commitment, appreciation of beauty, connection
- **Shadow:** Obsession, jealousy, loss of identity in relationship, addiction to intensity

### Creator
- **Healthy:** Innovation, self-expression, imagination, bringing visions to reality
- **Shadow:** Perfectionism, egotism, disconnection from reality, creating for ego

### Jester/Trickster
- **Healthy:** Joy, playfulness, living in moment, challenging conventions productively
- **Shadow:** Cruelty, manipulation, boundary violations, deception, irresponsibility

### Sage
- **Healthy:** Wisdom, truth-seeking, reflection, guidance, understanding
- **Shadow:** Dogmatism, ivory tower detachment, overthinking, judgmental coldness

### Magician
- **Healthy:** Transformation, healing, vision, making positive change
- **Shadow:** Manipulation, using power for selfish ends, deception, exploiting others

### Ruler
- **Healthy:** Leadership, responsibility, creating order, stability, prosperity
- **Shadow:** Tyranny, control obsession, authoritarianism, inability to delegate

## Other Important Archetypes

### Mother
- **Healthy:** Unconditional love, nurturing, protection, nourishment
- **Shadow:** Smothering, devouring, infantilizing, possessiveness, guilt manipulation

### Father
- **Healthy:** Protection, guidance, structure, teaching, healthy authority
- **Shadow:** Tyranny, rigidity, emotional unavailability, harsh judgment, abandonment

### Child
- **Healthy:** Spontaneity, joy, potential, new beginnings, innocence
- **Shadow:** Immaturity, dependency, tantrums, irresponsibility, narcissism

### Wise Old Man/Woman
- **Healthy:** Deep wisdom, guidance, spiritual insight, mentorship
- **Shadow:** Manipulation through "wisdom," dogmatic rigidity, senility

### Puer/Puella Aeternus (Eternal Boy/Girl)
- **Healthy:** Youthful spirit, creativity, potential, spontaneity
- **Shadow:** Refusal to grow up, avoiding commitment/responsibility, escapism, narcissism

## Additional Jungian Archetypes

### Great Mother
- **Healthy:** Life-giving force, cosmic nourishment, archetypal fertility, source of being
- **Shadow:** Devouring mother, death-bringing, overwhelming chaos, absorption into unconscious

### Divine Child
- **Healthy:** Miraculous potential, futurity, redemption, transformation, new consciousness
- **Shadow:** Vulnerability to destruction, helplessness, endangered innocence

### Senex (Old Man)
- **Healthy:** Wisdom of experience, tradition, discipline, order, tested knowledge
- **Shadow:** Rigidity, excessive conservatism, fear of change, spiritual death, sterility

### Syzygy (Divine Couple)
- **Healthy:** Union of opposites, completion, sacred marriage, integration of masculine/feminine
- **Shadow:** Loss of individual identity, codependency, failure to individuate

### Warrior
- **Healthy:** Discipline, courage, protecting boundaries, focused aggression, honor
- **Shadow:** Unnecessary violence, rigidity, emotional suppression, living only for battle

### Mentor
- **Healthy:** Teaching, preparing others, passing on knowledge, fostering growth
- **Shadow:** Living through students, refusing to let go, jealousy of youth, gatekeeping

### Threshold Guardian
- **Healthy:** Testing readiness, protecting sacred spaces, necessary obstacles for growth
- **Shadow:** Blocking legitimate passage, excessive gatekeeping, rigidity, fear-mongering

### Herald
- **Healthy:** Announcing change, bringing news, calling to adventure, catalyst
- **Shadow:** False alarms, crying wolf, manipulation through information, chaos-creation

### Shapeshifter
- **Healthy:** Adaptability, mystery, transformation, questioning assumptions
- **Shadow:** Deceitfulness, unreliability, destabilization, confusion for its own sake

### Maiden
- **Healthy:** Innocence, potential, fresh beginnings, youthful beauty, openness
- **Shadow:** Helplessness, dependency, perpetual victim, refusing growth

### Crone
- **Healthy:** Elder wisdom, acceptance of death, cutting truth, necessary endings
- **Shadow:** Bitterness, destructive criticism, death-wish for others, withholding wisdom

### Wild Man/Woman
- **Healthy:** Instinct, raw vitality, connection to nature, primal authenticity
- **Shadow:** Destructive chaos, regression, rejection of civilization, uncontrolled impulses

### Scapegoat
- **Healthy:** Carrying collective shadow for transformation, sacrifice for greater good
- **Shadow:** Victimhood, blame absorption, being used by collective, unjust persecution

### Hermit
- **Healthy:** Solitude for wisdom, inner journey, contemplation, spiritual seeking
- **Shadow:** Isolation, misanthropy, avoidance of life, fear of connection, spiritual pride

## Key Principle

Everyone contains all archetypes. Psychological health means accessing each appropriately while integrating their shadows.
